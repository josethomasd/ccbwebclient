About Us

College enquiry chatbot helps to get answer about course details and various college related queries using natural language processing and pattern matching techniques.

Various supported queries are:

  • Course information( Instructor, credits, syllabus, references, type etc.)
  • Time table
  • College contact information
  • General greetings
  • Data and time etc.
